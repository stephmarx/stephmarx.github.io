Hi there!

I am an aspiring web developer based out of Chicago, IL. From November 2014 to April 2015, I'm chasing my dreams and participating in DevBootcamp. If you have any words of wisdom or encouragement, I'd love to hear them!

My passion lies at the intersection of technology and roleplaying games. I love the use of the web to facilitate in-person interaction and gaming experiences. Scheduling app to decide when your next campaign session will be? Awesome! Forums to help you stay in-character between game sessions? Sounds good! Online character system to make building your next half-elf warrior a breeze? Sign me up!

I'm particularly itnerested in creating complete design solutions for games, from printable character sheets to rules reference mobile apps and from online character creation systems to old-school physical GM screens.
